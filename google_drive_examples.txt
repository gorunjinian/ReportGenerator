Google Drive URL Examples for CSV Files
=======================================

The program supports various Google Drive sharing link formats. 
Here are examples of supported formats:

1. Standard sharing link (most common):
   https://drive.google.com/file/d/1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs/view?usp=sharing

2. Open link format:
   https://drive.google.com/open?id=1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs

3. Direct file ID:
   1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs

4. Preview link:
   https://drive.google.com/file/d/1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs/preview

5. Download link:
   https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs

Multiple Images in CSV
======================
When you have multiple images in the "Additional images and files" field, 
separate them with commas:

https://drive.google.com/file/d/ABC123/view, https://drive.google.com/file/d/XYZ789/view, https://drive.google.com/file/d/QRS456/view

Important Notes
===============
- Make sure the images are set to "Anyone with the link can view" in Google Drive
- The program will skip images it cannot download and continue with the report
- Large images may take time to download depending on internet connection
- Supported image formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG
